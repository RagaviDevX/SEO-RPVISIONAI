## 🌟 7 Powerful AI Tools in One App

### 🎨 1. Text to Image
Type any prompt and instantly generate stunning AI masterpieces in seconds. We support 6 spectacular art styles including **Photorealistic, Cinematic, and Anime**.

### 🖼️ 2. Image to Image
Upload any image and let our AI reimagine it. Effortlessly change the style, mood, lighting, or completely overhaul the composition.

### 🎬 3. Text to Video
Generate cinematic video frames purely from text prompts! Perfect for professional storyboards, social media content, and visual presentations.

### 🎥 4. Image to Video
Bring your static photos to life. Upload any image and seamlessly add cinematic motion effects to create highly engaging video content.

### 🗣️ 5. Text to Audio
Convert text into natural-sounding speech instantly. The perfect tool for video voiceovers, accessibility features, and diverse content creation.

### 🔍 6. Image Upscaler
Got a blurry photo? Upscale any low-resolution image to **crystal clear HD quality** using deep AI learning. Perfect for printing and professional portfolios.

### 🪄 7. Remove Background
Instantly remove any image background with a single click. Get a perfect, transparent PNG immediately—no manual masking or editing required.
